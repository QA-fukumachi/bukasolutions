About Buka Solutions

Craftsmanship, transparency, and a commitment to transforming your space.

Our Story

Buka Solutions started with a background in building off-grid vans, tiny homes, and highly efficient alternative living spaces. That same spirit of innovation, extreme care for detail, and space optimization is brought to every home remodeling project we touch in Boulder, Colorado.

Our Values

We believe in transparent pricing, honest communication, and doing the job right the first time. We treat every home as if it were our own. From protecting your floors during a build to returning your calls promptly, respect for you and your property is our highest priority.

Our Experience

With years of hands-on experience covering everything from structural drywalling to intricate custom carpentry, there are very few problems we haven't seen—and solved. Whether we're designing built-in organization for a tiny home or gutting a sprawling kitchen, the quality of our work remains uncompromising.

The Build Process

From your first message to the final walkthrough — here's exactly what to expect.

Step 1

Submit Your Project

Share your ideas through the inquiry form. Include your plans, goals, and any details that help us understand the scope of work.

Step 2

We Review Your Inquiry

Every submission is personally reviewed. If your project is a good fit for Buka Solutions, we'll reach out to schedule an on-site walk-through.

Step 3

On-Site Walk-Through

Walk-throughs are priced at $125 or billed hourly. That cost is credited toward your final invoice when you move forward with us.

Step 4

You Receive a Detailed Estimate

After the walk-through, we provide a clear, itemized estimate covering labor, materials, and timeline — no vague numbers, no surprises.

Step 5

Secure Your Spot

A deposit of 15–30% (based on materials needed upfront) is required to add you to our build schedule.

Step 6

The Build Begins

Work starts on schedule. You'll receive regular progress updates and transparent cost reporting throughout the entire project.
